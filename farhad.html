About Us

Welcome to Yadgar Sweets and Baker, where we offer a wide variety of delicious sweets and baked goods made with the finest ingredients. Our family-owned business has been serving the community for over 50 years, and we take pride in the quality of our products and the satisfaction of our customers.

Our Menu

  • Gulab Jamun

    Our signature dessert made with soft, sweet dumplings soaked in a rose-scented syrup.

  • Ras Malai

    A creamy, indulgent dessert made with soft cheese balls in sweetened milk and garnished with nuts.

  • Chocolate Cake

    A classic chocolate cake made with rich cocoa powder and layered with chocolate frosting.

  • Cinnamon Rolls

    A warm and gooey treat, made with fluffy dough swirled with cinnamon and sugar, and topped with a sweet glaze.
